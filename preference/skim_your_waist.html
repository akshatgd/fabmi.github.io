Preference - Skim your Waist

DO'S:

  • Empire line dresses and tops that fall softly over the natural waist
  • Fitted shirts that nip in at the waist
  • Mid to high rise pants in firm fabrics
  • Pinstrip pants

DONT'S:

  • Very simple tops with no shape
  • Dresses with no shape at the waistline and high neckline
  • Coats and jackets without the belt and without the waistline
  • Pockets on your waist
  • Short boleros and cardigans

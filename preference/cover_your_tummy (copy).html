Preference - Cover your Tummy

DO'S:

  • Open necklines- scoop, V-neck, square or sweatheart necklines
  • Wrap tops/ dresses
  • Empire tops/ dresses that falls straight
  • Peplum tops
  • Ruching or draping that hides tummy
  • Vertical pleats
  • Fitted blazer/ jackets, prefer princess seams
  • Mid-rise to high-rise jeans or trouser
  • Tunics
  • Ballooned and banded tops

DONT'S:

  • Thin and clingy fabric
  • Loose and baggy clothes
  • Tucked in shirts
